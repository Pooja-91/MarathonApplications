1. 10KM Cycling

1.1. The event starts at 6:00am at necklace road near people’s plaza.
1.2. An amount of Rs 300 for below 25 years and Rs.400 for above 25 years should be made to participate in this event .
1.3. The participants should report 15mins prior to their given flag off time to collect their cycles.
1.4. Cycles will be issued only after the participant submits an original government ID (driving license, pan card, aadhar card or college id).
1.5. Once the cycle is issued the participant has to assemble near the start arch for their event.
1.6. Refreshments will be at the aid stations arranged on the route.
1.7. The participant has to collect their bibs, T-shirt and wrist band prior to the event day i. e 3rd April, 2016.
1.8. The amount shall not be transferred or refunded in case of cancellations or absence.
1.9. If the participant wish to slide to another event the extra amount has to be paid.
1.10. The maximum time to complete the 10Km cycling is 45 mins failing which the participant will be disqualified.
1.11. The participants are requested to return the cycles as soon as they complete their event and collect their id’s at the counter.
1.12. Participation certificates will be given only to those participants who complete cycling through the given route.
1.13. Any kind of cheating is not tolerated ,if found guilty the participant will be disqualified and will not be given certificate.
1.14. The participants should wear sports shoes, tracks.
